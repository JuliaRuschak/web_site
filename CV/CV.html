About me:

Hello everyone! My name is Julia, I am an interpreter by instruction. I speak Russian, English and Italian and one day I decided that learning a new language will never hurt.

Programming language is also a language, so in the beginning of 2023 having no experience in IT, leave alone front-end development, I've started to devote some of my free evenings to coding. It turned out to be even more fascinating that I could imagine, coding encroached on my weekends as well and here I am creating my own website to track my progress.

I am passionate about constant learning, so probably these first steps you can see on the right very soon will merge with my previous experience giving my career unexpected turn towards digital technologies.
